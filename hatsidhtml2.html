ABOUT PROFESSIONAL CRICKET ACADEMY


CricketMantras (CM) Academy is an academy with a clear intent to develop players professionally.The academy was founded in 2020, in peak lockdown and initially the coaching was online based due to the restrictionson ground training.What started with just one kid doing personal sessions has gradually evolved into a holistic Cricket Academy offering Cricket coaching, Strength and Conditioning, Nutrition and Yoga for mental conditioning of athletes.The academy now operates at 4 centres- 3 in Mumbai and 1 in Sangli and already has a school under its wings to facilitate opportunities for young players in prestigious inter school tournaments like Harris Shield, Giles Shield etc
